    Lesson:1 Nephi 8 Lehi's Vision of the Tree of Life

    January 23rd-24th

    Lehi's dream is all about a father who is trying to reach two of his wayward sons. President Boyd K. Packer taught the following: “You may think that Lehi’s dream or vision has no special meaning for you, but it does. You are in it; all of us are in it.” ~President Boyd K. Packer (Jan. 16th, ’07) As you search 1 Nephi chapter 8, I invite you to look for where you are in Lehi's dream. Try to imagine yourself as a loving parent whose son or daughter has lost their way. What truths do you discover that could help you reach out to those that are lost?
